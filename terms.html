FieldHero Decision Desk Terms

Effective date: July 16, 2026

Service

FieldHero Decision Desk provides remote decision support for experienced HVAC, handyman, and related trades professionals. Responses may include diagnostic direction, questions or checks, repair options, pricing guidance, customer explanations, and estimate or invoice wording.

Professional responsibility

FieldHero does not replace onsite inspection, testing, licensing, code compliance, manufacturer instructions, engineering, or the subscriber’s professional judgment. The onsite tradesperson remains solely responsible for safety, diagnosis, work methods, legal scope, pricing, and the final decision to proceed.

Membership

The Founding Membership is $59 USD billed monthly through PayPal until canceled. It includes four cases per billing cycle. Unused cases do not roll over. Additional cases are $20 each. One case covers one job or system and reasonable follow-up concerning the same decision. A different system, unrelated failure, or new service decision is a new case.

Response timing

Response times are targets rather than guarantees. FieldHero is not an emergency service. Situations involving immediate danger, fire, gas leaks, carbon monoxide, exposed energized conductors, refrigerant hazards, structural instability, flooding near electricity, or other urgent hazards require appropriate emergency or licensed local assistance.

Cancellation and refunds

Subscribers may cancel future billing through PayPal. Access continues through the current paid billing period unless the account is suspended for misuse, fraud, or safety concerns. Completed cases and partially used billing periods are generally nonrefundable, except where required by law or where FieldHero chooses otherwise.

Acceptable use

Users must provide accurate information and must not use FieldHero to evade licensing, code, environmental, safety, or legal requirements. FieldHero may refuse or stop a case when essential measurements are unavailable or the requested action is unsafe or outside appropriate scope.

Limitation

FieldHero provides good-faith decision support based on information supplied remotely. No diagnosis, result, savings, repair outcome, or revenue amount is guaranteed.
