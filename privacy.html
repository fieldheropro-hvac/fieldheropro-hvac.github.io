FieldHero Decision Desk Privacy Notice

Effective date: July 16, 2026

Information collected

FieldHero may receive names, business contact information, equipment and job details, photographs, measurements, case messages, PayPal payer names, subscription IDs, transaction references, and service feedback.

How information is used

Information is used to verify membership, deliver case support, communicate with users, track included cases, improve recurring templates, prevent fraud, and maintain business and tax records.

Payment information

Recurring payments are processed by PayPal. FieldHero does not receive or store complete debit-card, credit-card, or bank-account numbers through the website.

Job and customer data

Subscribers should send only information needed to analyze the job. Do not send Social Security numbers, full payment-card data, medical information, passwords, access codes, or unnecessary customer identifiers.

Sharing

FieldHero does not sell personal information. Information may be disclosed when required by law, needed to prevent fraud or harm, or necessary for vendors that support payment, communication, recordkeeping, or website operation.

Retention and security

Records are kept only as long as reasonably needed for service, business, tax, safety, or legal purposes. Reasonable safeguards are used, but no internet or messaging system can be guaranteed completely secure.

Requests

To ask about, correct, or request deletion of information that FieldHero controls, contact (937) 803-2556. Some records may need to be retained for payment, tax, dispute, safety, or legal reasons.
